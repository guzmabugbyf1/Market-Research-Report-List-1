<p><p>The Agricultural Irrigation Machinery Market primarily includes two key types: sprinkler irrigation and drip irrigation. Sprinkler irrigation uses a system of pipes and pumps to distribute water through spray heads, simulating natural rainfall over crops. Drip irrigation, on the other hand, delivers precise amounts of water directly to the plant roots through a network of tubing and emitters, minimizing waste and enhancing efficiency. Both methods aim to optimize water usage, improve crop yield, and address water scarcity challenges in agriculture.</p></p>
