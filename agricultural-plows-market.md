<p><p>The agricultural plow market features several key types, each catering to specific farming needs. The reversible plow allows for efficient soil turnover without the need for repositioning. Disc plows excel in breaking tough soil and managing residue, while ridge plows are designed for creating furrows for crop rows. Chisel plows focus on reducing soil compaction and improving aeration, and moldboard plows provide deep soil turning and mixing. Together, these plows facilitate diverse agricultural practices for improved productivity.</p></p>
