<p><strong>The Air Care Aerosol Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Phase Aerosol</li><li>Three-Phase Aerosol</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Care Aerosol market is primarily divided into two types: two-phase aerosols and three-phase aerosols. Two-phase aerosols consist of a liquid phase and a pressurized gas, allowing for a uniform dispersion of fragrance. In contrast, three-phase aerosols feature a liquid fragrance, water phase, and propellant, providing enhanced stability and longer-lasting scent release. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with two-phase aerosols often favored for their simplicity and three-phase aerosols valued for their complexity and efficacy.</p></p>
