<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Aircraft Hydraulic Actuators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Acting Drive</li><li>Double Acting Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft hydraulic actuators market is categorized into single-acting and double-acting drives. Single-acting drives utilize hydraulic pressure to move the actuator in one direction, relying on springs or gravity for return motion, making them simpler and lighter. Conversely, double-acting drives allow movement in both directions by applying pressure on both sides of the actuator, offering greater control and force. This distinction influences selection based on application requirements, weight considerations, and performance efficiency in various aircraft systems.</p></p>
