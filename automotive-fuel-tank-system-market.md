<p><strong>The Automotive Fuel Tank System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multilayer Fuel Tank</li><li>Single-layer Fuel Tank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive fuel tank system market comprises mainly two types: multilayer fuel tanks and single-layer fuel tanks. Multilayer fuel tanks are designed with several layers, providing enhanced barrier properties to prevent fuel evaporation and improve durability against external factors. In contrast, single-layer fuel tanks are simpler in construction, offering a cost-effective solution but with lower resistance to fuel permeation. The choice between these types often depends on regulatory requirements, cost considerations, and vehicle performance standards.</p></p>
