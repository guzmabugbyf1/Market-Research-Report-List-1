<p><p>The automotive intelligent headlight market encompasses various advanced lighting technologies aimed at enhancing visibility and safety. The Adaptive Front-lighting System (AFS) adjusts the headlight direction based on the vehicle's speed and steering angle, improving illumination during turns and in various driving conditions. Conversely, the Adaptive Driving Beam System (ADB) utilizes sensors to adapt the light distribution dynamically, providing maximum visibility without blinding oncoming drivers. Both systems significantly enhance driving experience and safety in diverse environments.</p></p>
