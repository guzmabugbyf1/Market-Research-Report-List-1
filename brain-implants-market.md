<p><p>The brain implants market encompasses various types of neurostimulation devices, primarily including deep brain stimulation (DBS), spinal cord stimulation (SCS), and vagus nerve stimulation (VNS). DBS is used for treating movement disorders and psychiatric conditions by delivering electrical impulses to specific brain regions. SCS targets chronic pain by stimulating the spinal cord. VNS involves stimulating the vagus nerve to manage epilepsy and depression. Each type caters to distinct neurological conditions, contributing to advancements in treating chronic ailments and enhancing quality of life.</p></p>
