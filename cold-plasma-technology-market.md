<p><p>The Cold Plasma Technology market is divided into two primary types: Low-Pressure and Atmospheric Pressure. Low-Pressure Cold Plasma operates in controlled environments with reduced pressure, making it suitable for applications like surface modification and sterilization of sensitive materials. In contrast, Atmospheric Pressure Cold Plasma functions at ambient conditions, allowing for easier integration in industrial processes. This technology is utilized in sectors such as healthcare, food safety, and materials science for its effectiveness in sterilization and enhancing surface properties without damaging substrates.</p></p>
