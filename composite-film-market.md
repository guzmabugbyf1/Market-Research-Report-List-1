<p><strong>The Composite Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autoclave</li><li>Out-of-autoclave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite film market can be divided into two primary types: autoclave and out-of-autoclave. The autoclave method involves curing composites under high pressure and temperature, ensuring uniform material properties and superior quality, making it suitable for critical applications in aerospace and automotive industries. In contrast, out-of-autoclave processing utilizes vacuum bags and ambient or moderate heat, offering a more cost-effective and flexible approach, often used in less critical industries such as sports equipment and consumer goods manufacturing.</p></p>
