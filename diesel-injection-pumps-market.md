<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Diesel Injection Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Distributor Pump</li><li>Individual Control Pump</li><li>Unit Injection</li><li>Common Rail System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diesel injection pumps market encompasses several key types: </p><p>1. **Rotary Distributor Pump**: This design distributes fuel to all cylinders simultaneously, using a rotating mechanism.</p><p>2. **Individual Control Pump**: Each cylinder has its pump, offering precise fuel delivery and timing.</p><p>3. **Unit Injection**: Combines fuel injection and pumping into a single unit per cylinder, enhancing efficiency.</p><p>4. **Common Rail System**: Utilizes a shared rail for high-pressure fuel delivery to injectors, enabling multiple injections per cycle for improved performance and emissions control.</p></p>
