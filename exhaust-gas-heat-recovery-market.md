<p><p>The Exhaust Gas Heat Recovery (EGHR) market primarily includes two types: heat exchangers and boilers. Heat exchangers capture waste heat from exhaust gases and transfer it to a working fluid, enhancing energy efficiency in industrial processes. Boilers, on the other hand, utilize this recovered heat to produce steam or hot water, supporting various applications, such as power generation and heating. Both technologies contribute to reducing energy consumption and emissions, driving sustainability in various sectors.</p></p>
