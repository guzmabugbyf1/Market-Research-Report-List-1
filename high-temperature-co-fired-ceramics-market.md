<p><p>The high-temperature co-fired ceramics (HTCC) market primarily involves two main types: Al2O3 HTCC boards and AlN HTCC boards. Al2O3 HTCC boards, made from aluminum oxide, offer excellent electrical insulation and thermal stability, making them suitable for various electronic applications. In contrast, AlN HTCC boards, which utilize aluminum nitride, provide superior thermal conductivity and are ideal for high-power and high-frequency devices. Both types are essential for advanced packaging solutions in the electronics and telecommunications industries.</p></p>
