<p><p>The home modular switch market comprises various types, including single control and dual control switches. A single control switch operates one electrical load independently, ideal for simple applications like controlling a single light fixture. In contrast, a dual control switch manages two loads, allowing for simultaneous control of multiple lights or devices from a single location. This versatility caters to diverse consumer needs, enhancing convenience and functionality in modern home setups. Both types contribute to energy efficiency and improved user experience.</p></p>
