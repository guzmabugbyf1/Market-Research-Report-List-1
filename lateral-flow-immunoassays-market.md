<p><p>Lateral Flow Immunoassays (LFIs) are simple, rapid diagnostic tests used to detect the presence of specific substances, typically biomolecules, in a liquid sample. These tests utilize a capillary action mechanism and are widely employed in various applications, including infectious disease testing, pregnancy detection, and environmental monitoring. LFIs offer advantages such as ease of use, portability, and the ability to deliver results within minutes, driving their popularity in both clinical and point-of-care settings.</p><p>The Lateral Flow Immunoassays Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period. The global pandemic has further accelerated the adoption of LFIs, as they provide quick and straightforward testing solutions for large populations. Recent trends also highlight the development of multi-analyte assays and enhanced detection methods, improving sensitivity and specificity. Furthermore, expanding applications in home healthcare and personalized medicine are likely to bolster market expansion.
