<p><p>The lithium-ion battery binders market comprises two main types: anode binders and cathode binders. Anode binders are used to hold together the anode materials and ensure mechanical stability, enhancing the overall performance and durability of the battery. Conversely, cathode binders serve a similar purpose for cathode materials, promoting adhesion and conductivity. Both binder types are crucial for optimizing the efficiency, energy capacity, and lifespan of lithium-ion batteries, contributing significantly to advancements in energy storage technologies.</p></p>
