<p><p>The metal cleaning machinery market encompasses two primary types: solvent metal cleaning equipment and aqueous metal cleaning equipment. Solvent metal cleaning equipment utilizes organic solvents to effectively remove oils and contaminants from metal surfaces, offering a quick drying process. In contrast, aqueous metal cleaning equipment employs water-based solutions, often combined with detergents or surfactants, to clean metals more environmentally friendly. Both types are essential for various industries, ensuring metal parts are prepared for further processing or finishing.</p></p>
