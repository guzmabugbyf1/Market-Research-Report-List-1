<p><p>The Oil & Gas Defoaming Separator market consists of various types designed to efficiently separate foam from liquid. Horizontal baffles utilize gravity to remove foam across multiple stages. Parallel plates enhance separation by increasing the surface area for foam collapse. Random packing facilitates foam reduction by providing a large internal surface area for interaction.
