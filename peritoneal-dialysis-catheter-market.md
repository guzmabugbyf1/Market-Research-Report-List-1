<p><ul><li>Straight Catheter</li><li>Pigtail-curled Catheter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The peritoneal dialysis catheter market features two primary types: straight catheters and pigtail-curled catheters. Straight catheters have a simple, linear design that facilitates easy insertion and can be used for various dialysis procedures. In contrast, pigtail-curled catheters have a coiled tip that prevents migration and helps maintain optimal placement within the peritoneal cavity. These differences influence their applications, patient comfort, and overall effectiveness in peritoneal dialysis treatments, driving demand in the healthcare market.</p></p>
