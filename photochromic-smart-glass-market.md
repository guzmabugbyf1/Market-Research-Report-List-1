<p><p>The Photochromic Smart Glass market comprises primarily two types: Cadmium Oxide (CdO) Glass and Silver Halide (AgX) Glass. CdO Glass is known for its quick response to UV light, providing effective tinting and clarity management. In contrast, AgX Glass utilizes silver halides that react to light changes, allowing for a smoother transition and superior performance in varying light conditions. Both types enhance energy efficiency and comfort in buildings and vehicles, adapting to environmental changes seamlessly.</p></p>
