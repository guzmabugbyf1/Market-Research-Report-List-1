<p><ul><li>Volumetric Pump Feeders</li><li>Gravitational Pump Feeders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pump feeders are systems designed to deliver precise quantities of materials, commonly used in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals. </p><p>Volumetric pump feeders measure and control the flow of materials based on their volume, ensuring accuracy and consistency. In contrast, gravitational pump feeders rely on gravity to move materials from a higher to a lower point, allowing for a more straightforward setup but potentially less precision. Both types are crucial for maintaining quality and efficiency in various production processes.</p></p>
