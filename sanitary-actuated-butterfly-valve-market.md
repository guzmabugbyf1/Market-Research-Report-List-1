<p><p>The Sanitary Actuated Butterfly Valve market comprises three primary types: Manual, Electric, and Pneumatic. Manual type valves are operated through a hand wheel or lever, allowing for straightforward control in low-pressure applications. Electric type valves use electric actuators for precise and automated operation, ideal for processes requiring frequent adjustments. Pneumatic type valves utilize compressed air for actuation, offering rapid response and high reliability in dynamic environments. Each type serves distinct applications in industries such as food, beverage, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
