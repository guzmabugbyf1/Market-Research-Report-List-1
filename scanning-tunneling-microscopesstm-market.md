<p><strong>The Scanning Tunneling Microscopes（STM） Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant-height Mode</li><li>Constant-current Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Scanning Tunneling Microscopes (STM) are advanced tools for imaging surfaces at the atomic level, utilizing quantum tunneling of electrons between a sharp tip and the sample. In Constant-Height Mode, the tip maintains a fixed height while scanning, ideal for flat surfaces to measure slight changes in conductivity. Conversely, Constant-Current Mode adjusts the tip's height to maintain a constant tunneling current, providing detailed topographic maps of varied surface features. Both modes cater to different research scenarios in nanotechnology and materials science.</p></p>
