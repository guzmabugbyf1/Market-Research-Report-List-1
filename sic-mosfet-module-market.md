<p><ul><li>Full Silicon Carbide Modules</li><li>Hybrid Silicon Carbide Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SiC MOSFET module market consists of two main types: Full Silicon Carbide Modules and Hybrid Silicon Carbide Modules. Full Silicon Carbide Modules are entirely made of SiC components, offering superior efficiency, thermal performance, and high-frequency operation, making them ideal for demanding applications. Conversely, Hybrid Silicon Carbide Modules combine SiC MOSFETs with traditional silicon components, balancing performance and cost, and making them suitable for less demanding applications where budget constraints are a consideration.</p></p>
