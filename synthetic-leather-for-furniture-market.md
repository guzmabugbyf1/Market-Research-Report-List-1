<p><p>Synthetic leather for furniture encompasses various types, including PVC, Normal PU, Microfiber PU, and Ecological Function PU. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) offers a budget-friendly option with decent durability but limited breathability. Normal PU (polyurethane) is softer and more flexible, providing a leather-like feel. Microfiber PU has a dense and breathable structure, mimicking natural leather while being lightweight. Ecological Function PU emphasizes sustainability, utilizing eco-friendly materials and production methods, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers in modern furniture markets.</p></p>
