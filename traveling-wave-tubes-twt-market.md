The market includes two primary types: Helix TWTs, characterized by their helical structure that supports wideband amplification and high efficiency, and Coupled Cavity TWTs, which utilize a series of cavities to enhance gain and bandwidth. Both types cater to different frequency ranges and operational requirements, making them essential in modern telecommunications and electronic warfare.</p></p>
