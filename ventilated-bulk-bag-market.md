<p><p>The Ventilated Bulk Bag market is categorized into Two Sides Strip Fabrics and Four Sides Strip Fabrics. Two Sides Strip Fabrics feature ventilation strips on two sides, allowing for efficient airflow, ideal for products sensitive to moisture and temperature. In contrast, Four Sides Strip Fabrics have ventilation strips on all four sides, offering enhanced airflow and breathability, suitable for a wider range of applications. Both types facilitate the safe storage and transportation of bulk materials, improving product quality.</p></p>
