<p><ul><li>Clip-on Tuner</li><li>Dial Tuner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The violin tuner market primarily features two types: clip-on tuners and dial tuners. Clip-on tuners are portable and attach directly to the instrument, utilizing built-in microphones or vibration sensors for quick, accurate tuning feedback. They are favored by many for their convenience and ease of use. Dial tuners, on the other hand, offer a traditional approach with a needle gauge indicating pitch accuracy, appealing to those who prefer a more visual and tactile tuning experience. Both types cater to the diverse needs of musicians.</p></p>
